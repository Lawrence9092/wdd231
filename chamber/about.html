About the Harare Chamber of Commerce

Founded in 1985, the Harare Chamber of Commerce serves as a unifying body for local businesses, advocating for sustainable economic growth and providing networking opportunities across industries.

Our Vision

To be the leading advocate for business innovation, community growth, and economic empowerment in Zimbabwe.

Our Leadership

Our board is composed of business leaders from finance, manufacturing, hospitality, and technology sectors — all dedicated to shaping a thriving business environment in Harare.
